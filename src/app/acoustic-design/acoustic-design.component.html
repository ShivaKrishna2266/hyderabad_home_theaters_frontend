Acoustic Design

Why Acoustics Matter?

The sound you hear is a mix of direct sound and reflected sound. Reflections enrich the experience but too many can create echoes or muddiness. We use acoustic panels, bass traps, and diffusers to control these reflections and deliver clear, immersive sound, letting you hear every detail as the creators intended.

Soundproofing

Keep the Sound In

Great home theatres don’t just sound amazing inside — they don’t disturb the rest of the house. We install mass-loaded vinyl layers, acoustic doors, insulated ceilings, and carpeted floors to block sound escape and keep external noise from ruining your movie night.

Seating Layout

Comfort + Perfect Angles

We design seating that maximizes comfort and ensures every seat is in the audio and video sweet spot. Whether it’s tiered recliners, motorized chairs, or custom benches, we make sure your setup provides luxury comfort and the best sightlines.

Screen & Projector Choices

Vivid, Razor-Sharp Visuals

Depending on your space, we help you choose the perfect fixed or motorized screen and match it with top-tier 4K or 8K projectors. We ensure cinematic picture quality, whether you’re watching movies, sports, or gaming.

Ambient Lighting

Set the Mood

Lighting transforms your theatre. We add dimmable LED strips, fiber optic star ceilings, and smart lighting controls so you can set the perfect atmosphere with one tap, all while avoiding unwanted screen glare.
