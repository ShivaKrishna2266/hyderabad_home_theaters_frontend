Acoustic Design

Why Acoustics Matter?

The sound you hear in any room is a combination of the direct sound that travels straight from your speakers to your ears, and the indirect reflected sound — the sound that bounces off the walls, floors, ceiling, and furniture before it reaches you. These reflections make music and dialogue sound much fuller and louder, adding a pleasant spaciousness that enhances your overall experience.
