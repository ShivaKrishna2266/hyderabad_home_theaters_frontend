Acoustic Design

Why Acoustics Matter?

The sound you hear in any room is a combination of the direct sound that travels straight from your speakers to your ears, and the indirect reflected sound the sound from your speakers that bounces off the walls, floors, ceiling and furniture before it reaches your ears. The good part is that they make music and movie dialogue sound much fuller and louder than they would otherwise. Reflected sound can add a pleasant spaciousness to your sound.
